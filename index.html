GET THE MUSIC

Do you live to dance? Need a dose of endorphins before you head back to the library? Want to get pumped before your next evening activity?

Silent Rave is your chance to GET WEIRD in a quiet room with a bunch of acquaintances. Download our mix onto your favorite MP3 or music player (or just hit up SoundCloud) and bring your headphones. We'll all hit play together and rock out.

Dress code includes appropriate dancing clothes, which include but are not limited to leotards, crop tops, jungle costumes, bangles, frou-frou, minimalism, excessive scarves, wands, cat ears, or whatever you wore to class.
